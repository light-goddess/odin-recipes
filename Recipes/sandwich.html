Sandwich

Description

I am going to be making a simple but tasty peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Read this page if you want to figure out how!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Jam
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Take your bread slices and pop them in your toaster
  2. When they are done toasting, take them out of the toaster and put them in a bowl or on a plate
  3. Take your Jam and spread it on a slice of bread
  4. Take your peanut butter and spread it on the other
  5. Eat and Enjoy!
