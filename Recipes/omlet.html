Sandwich

Description

I am going to be making an omlet. A delicious omlet that I love.

Read this page and follow the steps if you want to make one as well!

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 eggs
  • Some seasonings(powders)

Steps

  1. Crack your egg(or eggs) in the pan
  2. Sprinkle your seasonings on the omlet
  3. Wait a few minutes and flip the omlet
  4. Wait a few more minutes then take it off the pan to cool
  5. Eat it(or save it for later)
